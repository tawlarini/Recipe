Chicken Strognoff

Ingredients

  • Chopped Chicken
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cream Fraiche
  • Onions
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Add the chopped garlic, onions and oil in the pan.
  2. Add the chicken
  3. When the chicken is well-done, add the tomato sauce
  4. After 20 minutes, add the cream fraiche
  5. You can add seasoning, salt and mustard or improve with your preferences
